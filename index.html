The Three Pillars

One ecosystem.
Three engines of change.

Every pillar feeds the next. Land produces crops for the store. Large farms supply the same market. One closed loop — no leakage, no brokers.

01
Land Exchange
Idle land.
Active farms.

Urban landowners with idle farmland match with verified farmers. Monthly or long-term leases. Trained workers included. Full legal documentation handled.

AgriConnect earns: 8–12% commission
02
Direct Marketplace
Farm to table.
Zero brokers.

Farmers bring produce directly to AgriConnect stores. Reaches restaurants, hotels, and consumers. Cost-plus pricing — no inflation, no crashes. The farmer always wins.

AgriConnect earns: 15–18% store margin
03
Agri Partnership
Own land.
No resources?

For farmers with large land but no capital, AgriConnect deploys technology and trained workers. Farmer earns monthly salary or profit split on final crop value.

AgriConnect earns: 20% crop value split

5 Unique Innovations

Built to last.
Not just to launch.

🏆
AgriScore™

Proprietary farmer credit rating from yield history, land care, and platform behaviour. Unlocks microloans, insurance, and lease priority.

⚖️
Price Stability Engine

Cost-plus pricing model requiring both supply and distribution. No competitor has this — no farmer suffers a price crash ever again.

🎙️
Vernacular AI App

Voice-first AI in Kannada, Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu for low-literacy farmers. Ask about weather, sowing, pests — in your mother tongue.

📱
QR Organic Certification

Every batch QR-coded with farm origin, farmer photo, soil report, zero-chemical pledge. Consumers know exactly who grew their food.

🍽️
AgriConnect Kitchens

B2B SaaS for restaurants at locked-in weekly prices. Predictable procurement. Zero price surprises. Zero brokers.

🛡️
PMFBY Digiclaim

Farmers auto-file flood claims via the app. Satellite confirms → auto payout in 2 weeks. No inspector. No wait. No loss of dignity.
